F4 Thailand:Boys Over Flowers

Synopsis

The story is about Gorya, a girl from a poor family who is able to go to an elite high school through a scholarship. She secretly despises the school's open secret, which is a free-for-all bullying campaign towards anyone who gets a "red card" from the F4 led by Thyme.

Characters

Gorya Thithara Jundee

the main female lead of the series. She is brave, kind and headstrong. She was admitted into a prestigious school. She works at a flower shop with her best friend Kaning. She becomes the first person in her school to stand up against the bullies, F4 from bullying her friend. After angering Thyme, she receives the 'Red Card' from F4. Initially, she had feelings for Ren, another F4 member who saved her many times. However, as the series goes on, she is moved by Thyme's sincerity, she starts liking him and then she falls in love with him.

Thyme Akira Paramaanantra

The main male lead of the series. He is an arrogant and short-tempered leader of F4 but deep inside he is warm. He comes from a wealthy and domineering family. His bullying ways causes everybody in the school to fear him. He is always childish as his parents were absent for most of the time in his life. He is surprised by Gorya's bravery and is smitten by her. He strives to protect Gorya at all costs and is willing to go to extreme ends to keep her safe as he has fallen in love with her.

Ren Renrawin Aira

The talented and quiet member of F4. Also from a wealthy family, Ren has been friends with Thyme since they were young children. He is in love with his childhood friend, Mira, who befriended him from a young age despite his quiet and withdrawn demeanour. Later, he develops feelings for Gorya.

Kavin Taemiyaklin Kittiyangkul

The playboy member of F4. He is also a charming and prolific playboy, due to an unresolved past with his first love, Mona. He later expresses interest in Gorya's best friend, Kaning.

MJ Methas Jarustiwa

The mood-maker member of F4. He is lighthearted, enjoys making jokes, and has an incredible memory. Like Kavin, he is a flirtatious casanova.He is good football player. He love interest with Aum
